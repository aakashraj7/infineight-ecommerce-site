Our Services

We provide curated collections of top-tier gadgets and accessories, real-time customer support, secure payment systems, and fast doorstep delivery across the country. Our platform is designed to offer a hassle-free experience from product search to final checkout.

Consumer Policy

We are committed to ethical business practices and total transparency. Our consumer policy ensures that your data is safe, transactions are secure, and your satisfaction is our top priority. We also ensure strict compliance with all e-commerce regulations.

Licence and Verifications

InfinEight is a legally registered business operating with valid licenses. All listed products are sourced from verified vendors and come with quality assurance. Certifications and legal compliance documents can be provided upon request.

Shipping

We offer reliable shipping across India, with tracking options and estimated delivery times provided upon checkout. Standard and express delivery options are available.

Return Policy

Not happy with your product? Our easy return policy allows for returns within 7 days of delivery. Products must be in unused condition with original packaging.

Order Procedures

Placing an order is simple: browse > add to cart > checkout > receive confirmation. Our intuitive interface and customer support will guide you every step of the way.

Payment Options

Choose from a wide range of secure payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) in select regions.
